An Interesting Blowfly Specimen in the UOG Insect Collection


Fig, 1. Screenshot from https://www.idigbio.org/wiki/images/a/aa/Scratchpads_iDigBio-part1.pdf.

The University of Guam Insect collection contaims a pinned specimen of a blowfly (Fig. 1). This specimen is apparently from a study of insect succession in human corpses. This study is reported in Filth-inhabiting Flies of Guam (G. E. Bohart and J. L. Gressitt 1951) and it is also described in Life on a Little Known Planet (H. E. Evans 1968) (Fig. 2).
